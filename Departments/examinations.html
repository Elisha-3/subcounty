Examination Department

The Examination Department of the Sub County Education Office is committed to maintaining the highest standards of academic assessment and integrity. Our primary role is to coordinate the planning, administration, and supervision of both internal and national examinations across all schools within the sub-county. We ensure that all examination processes are conducted fairly, transparently, and efficiently, from registration to the release of results. Through collaboration with schools, we strive to provide accurate and timely data to guide student progression and uphold the credibility of our education system.

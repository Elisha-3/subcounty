ICT Department

The ICT Department at the Sub County Education Office plays a critical role in enhancing the digital infrastructure of the education sector. We are dedicated to supporting the integration of technology in schools and ensuring that educators, students, and administrators have access to reliable and efficient IT services. Our responsibilities include maintaining the digital learning environment, managing data systems, providing technical support, and facilitating training on new technologies. By leveraging innovative solutions, we strive to improve the delivery of education and foster an environment where technology empowers learning and administration across the sub-county.

Our Mission

"To enhance the quality of education by providing innovative, efficient, and reliable ICT solutions that support teaching, learning, and administrative functions within the sub-county."

Our Vision

"To be a leader in the integration of technology in education, fostering an environment where digital tools and resources empower educators, students, and administrators for academic and operational excellence."

Sub-county Education

Providing, promoting and coordinating quality education, training, researching and enhancing integration of Science Technology and Innovation into national production system for sustainable development.

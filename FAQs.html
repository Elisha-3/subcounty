Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Get answers to the most commonly asked questions about the Nyamira South Sub-County Education Office.

You can contact the Sub-County Education Office via email at scdenyamira@gmail.com or by calling +254 781504768. You can also visit our office located in Nyamira town at the address provided on the Contact Us page.

Enrollment is done directly through the respective schools. You can contact the schools listed on our Institutions page for more information on admission procedures. For specific guidance, you can also reach out to the Sub-County Education Office.

The Resources page contains materials for teachers and students, including curriculum guides, examination past papers, educational policies, and online learning resources.

You can report an issue or file a complaint directly with the Sub-County Education Office. Visit our Contact Us page for details on how to submit your concerns via email, phone, or by visiting our office in person.

Examination results are handled by the schools directly. However, the Sub-County Education Office may provide oversight and support. Please contact the respective school or the office for further guidance.

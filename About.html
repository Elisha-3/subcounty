About Us

Nyamira South Sub-county is found in West Mugirango, Nyamira County and Nyanza region. It's located 100M from Huduma Center Nyamira.

Further, the Sub-county is divided into 5 wards namely: Bogichora, Bosamaro, Bonyamatuta, Township and Nyamaiya Wards

It has 99 Public Primary Institutions, 3 SNE schools, 51 Public Secondary Schools and 3 colleges

We are dedicated to providing a better Education Experience.

Our Mission

To Provide, promote and coordinate quality education, training, research and enhancing integration of Science Technology and Innovation into national production system for sustainable development.

Our Vision

A globally competitive education, trainng and research system for sustainable development
